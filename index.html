Project Overview

River Runner (Survey123 edition) is a prototype tool for documenting river conditions using ESRI's Survey123. It supports community-driven reporting of invasive species, erosion, blockages, and more.

How to Use the Survey

  1. Open the River Runner Survey web form
  2. Select the type of observation and upload a photo.
  3. Fill in the key characteristics based on what you observed.
  4. Use the map to mark the location and submit your report.

Observation Types

  • Invasive/Non-Native Species: Dropdown with species, plus notes.
  • Riverbank Erosion: Severity, bank side, vegetation cover, notes.
  • Trash or Dumping: Type, location, size.
  • Snag Piles / Log Jams: Size, hazard level, visibility.
